Who We Are

Built by Veterans.
Trusted by Operators.

Strategic Task Solutions, LLC (STS) is a Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business headquartered in Merritt Island, Florida. We provide mission-ready defense consulting, professional services, and cleared key personnel to U.S. Government prime contractors, DoD and DoW agencies, Federal agencies, and State agencies.

Our leadership team has stood up organizations from concept to full operational capability, accelerated revenue growth, and managed full P&L accountability across ISR fleet operations supporting DoD and Federal customers across all branches and commands. We've operated in austere combat environments and directed enterprise-level programs — bringing that same standard of execution to every engagement.

STS bridges the gap between operational experience and corporate execution — delivering solutions that are both tactically sound and professionally precise. Our size is our advantage — we move at a pace and with a level of flexibility that most companies simply cannot match. From fires integration and Full Spectrum ISR Ecosystem development to international partner capacity building, curriculum development, and business development capture, our team has done it at the highest levels.

Our leadership team also brings deep executive experience supporting startups and small businesses — from organizational structuring and scaling to leadership mentoring and brand program development aligned to government contracting and business growth objectives. We understand what it takes to build something from the ground up and sustain it.

SDVOSB — A Competitive Advantage

STS is a verified Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Business — and that's more than a certification. It means you're partnering with a team that brings operationally proven leadership, active clearances, and real execution capability to every engagement. Our SDVOSB status enables prime contractors to meet set-aside and subcontracting requirements while gaining a high-performing partner who delivers. For Government customers, STS offers a streamlined path to mission-critical expertise through VA sole-source awards and DoD set-aside vehicles — without sacrificing capability or capacity.
